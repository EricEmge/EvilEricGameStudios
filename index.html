Hochone is a 2D Multiplayer game where 1 player is the seeker while the rest are Hochs trying to blend in with society. Will you be able to stay hidden in the crowd or will you be outed by the seeker? Play as a Hoch who are people who don't belong in society, try and blend in till the time is up, or become the seeker and try to figure out all the Hochs before the time runs out. Play in 2D maps with various unique mechanics in this non-traditional role-playing game about deception and trying to blend in.
